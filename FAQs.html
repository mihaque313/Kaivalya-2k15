Questions presently in your mind


Q. Who can join this club?
Ans. Any student of BTKIT, who is aspiring to excel in programming, web designing, or any other field of computer science.

Q. How to resister for this club?
Ans. Simple! Just go to the 'resister here' link and you are few minutes away from being registered online. After that you have to the confirmation letter from the office of
HOD, CSE Department.

Q. Is there any registration fee for joining WoCS?
Ans. Absolutely not! It is totally free for all the students and alumni of the college. If any one ask for the payment please report to the HOD Office.

Q. Will the alumni also have to get confirmation from HOD office?
Ans. No, only thing they have to do is to mail their degree to the e-mail address given in contact section below.

Q. Can an alumni financially help this club?
Ans. you are welcome to it.Simply send a cheque/draft in the name of 'WoCS-BTKIT' payable at dwarahat/almora.
