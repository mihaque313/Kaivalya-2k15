-:Wizards of Computer Science, a short intro:-

We are the computing club of BTKIT, the heaven for the Geeks amidst the lush green hilly mountains of Dwarahat Range in Kumaon Region.

Wizards of Computer Science, one of its kind, being mentored by Mr K S Viasla, HOD, Computer Science Department at BTKIT ,Dwarahat has had a long-standing reputation of outstanding education program enabling students to grow with and adopt to rapid changing technology. The computing club of CSE department is maintained by the students undergoing the courses of B.Tech and MCA in the institute. .

Looking to become an expert in your chosen field? if so, you are in the right place. Register here, and take your first step to become nation's top computing programmer.
